Known Issues:
- DirectInput EnumDevices hangs on some systems. If "joy.cpl" also hangs, this is the issue
- AVX2 detection does not work correctly under WINE, support is assumed.
  if running an application compiled with REQUIRE_AVX2 under WINE on a system without AVX2 support, expect a crash
